## 1. Safety First (The "Don't Verify Physics with Your Body" Rule)
This curriculum involves hardware that interacts with the real world.
- **Electricity**: 3.3V is generally safe, but short circuits can get **hot**.
- **Lasers**: Never point laser modules at eyes (human or animal).
- **Fire**: Flame sensors detect fire. Don't start uncontrolled fires indoors to test them. Use a lighter or a candle safely.
